About MBA

MBA full form is Master of Business Administration. Oxford languages define business administration as "a course of study at a university or college that prepares students for managerial roles in companies or organizations." MBA duration is two years and suitable for graduates who want to pursue their career as entrepreneurs, business executives, technocrats, and managers. This course is especially for aspirants who want to equip exhaustive knowledge on management and principles. It focuses on various subjects like marketing, entrepreneurial learning, organizational behavior, and human resource management, and much more.
